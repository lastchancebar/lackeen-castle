Lackeen Castle -treasure of North Tipperary

Built in the 12th Century

This tower was built around the time that the Normans invaded Ireland. Nearly 1000 years on, you can still climb to the top to see the green fields that surrond Lackeen.

The World famous O'Kennedy Clan

The O'Kennedy/ Kennedy family descended from kings and are well-known in politics and the arts across the world.
