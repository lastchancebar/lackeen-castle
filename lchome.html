About Lackeen Castle

When was it built?

Lackeen was built in the 12th century by the O'Kennedy family, possibly to repel Norman invaders. The tower standing now was rebuilt in the 16th century. It kept some scary defensive features. Read on.....

Stowe Missal

The castle was refurbished again in the 18th century. A priceless family prayer book was found hidden in the castle wall. This manuscript, called the Stowe Missal is kept in the Royal Irish Academy and contains prayers, Gospels and cures for ailments! It has a spectacular cover box or clúdach.

The O Kennedys

The O'Kennedy's are significant on a world scale. They are descended from Cinn Éide, a nephew of Brian Ború, high king of Ireland (d.1014). They are ancestors of John F. Kennedy, 35th US president, who made a huge contribution to world history.
