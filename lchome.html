Lackeen - the O'Kennedy fortress

  • This imposing tower in North Tipperary was built nearly 1000 years ago by the O'Kennedys, a family that trace their roots to Brian Ború, high king of Ireland. John F. Kennedy, 35th US President, is a descendent..
  • The castle has some interesting features to deter enemy invaders. Click here to find out more.
  • A priceless family prayer book- the Stowe Missal a manuscript of national importance, was found in the hidden castle walls 300 years ago. Ir was found in a beautifully ornate box or clúdach .

How to get to Lackeen

Planes

From Dublin, take M7 (south). From Shannon, take M7 (East).

Trains

Nearest stations are Nenagh and Roscrea.

Automobiles

From Athlone, follow the L236 to Limerick.
