Lackeen Castle -treasure of North Tipperary

1000 years old

Built at the time that the Normans invaded Ireland. You can still climb to the top to see the green fields that surrond Lackeen.

The O'Kennedys

The O'Kennedy family descended from kings and are well-known in politics and the arts across the world.

Stowe Missal

A priceless manuscript - the Stowe Missal -was found hidden in the castle walls 300 years ago. The book was written nearby over 1,000 years ago, and has a beautifully ornate storage box or reliquary.
