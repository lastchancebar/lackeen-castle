The Stowe Missal

Manuscript of national importance

Stowe Missal -treasure found in the castle walls

Origin

This diminutive 11th century manuscript in its ornate reliquary or storage box, was found in the walls of Lackeen Castle during its renovation in the 1700's.


The 15 cm tall book contains the Mass, Sacrament prayers, St John's Gospel in Latin, and as an added extra, three healing charms in Gaelic, one for the prick of a thorn!

The Missal Today

The Stowe Missal is in the safe keeping of the Royal Irish Academy in Dublin.

The pages can be viewed online and are the subject of much scholarly discussion.


Lorrha Historical Society are in discussions with the RIA to allow the manuscript to be exhibited in Lackeen Castle.
